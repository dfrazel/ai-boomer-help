Accessibility Statement

Last updated: July 4, 2026

AI Boomer Help is committed to making aiboomerhelp.com usable by everyone, including people who use screen readers, keyboard navigation, or other assistive technology. Accessibility matters especially for the community I serve, and I take it seriously.

Standards I Follow

I aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, the widely recognized standard for web accessibility. This includes efforts such as:

  • High-contrast black-and-white design for readability
  • Text that can be navigated and activated using only a keyboard
  • Descriptive text on links and buttons rather than vague labels like "click here"
  • Alternative text on images so screen readers can describe them
  • A "Skip to main content" option for keyboard and screen reader users

Ongoing Work

Accessibility is not a one-time fix, and I continue to review and improve the site over time. Some elements on this site, such as the payment checkout, are provided by a third-party processor (Stripe); I encourage that provider to meet the same standards, though I do not control their code directly.

Let Me Know

If you use assistive technology and run into any barrier on this site, or if you have suggestions on how I can make it easier to use, please reach out. I will do my best to address it promptly and personally — no automated ticket system, just me.
